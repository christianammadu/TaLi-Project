How it works

Three texts. Clean books.

You already know how to send a message. That's the whole skill. Here's what happens between your text and your books.

sold 2 bags rice 5,000 to mama nkechi on credit
Got it ✓ ₦5,000 sale, 2 bags off stock, ₦5,000 added to Mama Nkechi's tab.
Step 01 — Message it

Say it like you'd say it.

Plain words, pidgin, shorthand — even two things in one message. No menus, no categories to pick, no forms.

Step 02 — TaLi understands

It pulls out the details — and checks them.

Amount, type, item, category and date are extracted, then validated before saving. Anything unusual (a very large amount, a possible duplicate) pauses for your “yes” first.
