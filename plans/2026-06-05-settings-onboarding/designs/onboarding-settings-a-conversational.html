● Variant A — Conversational · text-first

Build cost
Lowest. Reuses the existing text-command dispatcher (routes.py) and send_reply. No new send-side code, no Meta review.
Reach
Universal. Plain text renders identically on Android, iOS and WhatsApp Web/desktop.
Trade-off
More typing for the user; menus are text, not tappable. Numbered choices can feel clunky for long option lists.
