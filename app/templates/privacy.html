Privacy Policy

Last updated: 3 June 2026

TaLi ("we", "us") helps you keep business records through WhatsApp and Telegram. This policy explains what we collect, why, and the choices you have. We aim to collect only what we need to run the service.

1. Information we collect

To provide TaLi we collect:

  • Your WhatsApp number or Telegram account — used to link and authenticate your account.
  • The messages you send TaLi — the transaction, inventory and query text you choose to record.
  • Derived bookkeeping data — amounts, categories, dates and stock levels extracted from your messages.
  • Basic usage logs — timestamps and delivery status, used for reliability and support.

We do not read your other WhatsApp or Telegram chats. We only process messages sent directly to TaLi.

2. How we use your information

  • To record, categorise and report on your transactions and inventory.
  • To authenticate you and keep your account secure.
  • To send you the summaries and replies you request.
  • To improve reliability and provide support when you contact us.

3. AI processing

To understand natural language, the text you send is processed by a third-party language model provider strictly to extract bookkeeping details. This text is not used to train external models, and is transmitted over encrypted connections.

4. Sharing

We do not sell your data. We share information only with service providers who help us operate TaLi (messaging, hosting, language processing) under confidentiality obligations, or where required by law.

5. Data & security

Your records are private to your account and locked to your phone number. We use encryption in transit and access controls. No system is perfectly secure, but we work to protect your information and to notify you of material incidents.

6. Your rights

In line with the Nigeria Data Protection Act and similar laws, you may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your data, and you may withdraw consent at any time by messaging delete my account or contacting support.

7. Contact

Questions about privacy? Reach us at privacy@tali.app.
